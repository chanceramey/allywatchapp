A smartwatch app that can save lives.

AllyWatch is a smartwatch application that provides health monitoring services for the elderly. Unlike products like life alert, AllyWatch is inconspicuous and a provides a broad range of features including heart rate monitoring, easy SOS emergency dialing, remote monitoring of loved ones through an online platform, live GPS tracking for those with conditions that affect memory, and more.
